About Us

SK Engineering Consultancy is a reliable and innovative civil engineering firm located in Bharatpur, Chitwan, Nepal. We specialize in residential house map design, structural planning, 3D modeling, and construction supervision. With a focus on safety, sustainability, and creativity, we are committed to transforming your vision into a strong and elegant reality. Our experienced team combines technical expertise with personalized service to ensure every project meets the highest standards of quality and client satisfaction.

Our Mission

To provide top-quality civil engineering solutions that combine innovation, safety, and sustainability for residential and commercial projects.
