About Us

SK Engineering Consultancy is a reliable and innovative civil engineering firm based in Bharatpur, Chitwan, Nepal. We specialize in house map design, structural planning, 3D modeling, and construction supervision.

Our mission is to deliver sustainable and aesthetic building solutions with a balance of design, durability, and cost-effectiveness. We are committed to turning your dream spaces into reality with professionalism and precision.

Our Mission

We believe in building homes and relationships that last.
